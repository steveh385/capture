View of Aegina

This is the island of Aegina. We ended up taking a ferry from Athens port for a day trip to Aegina. The ferry took a little over an hour where we passed many beautiful Greek islands.
When we arrived to the island we walked around the village and grabbed lunch along the marina. Plenty of options to choose from. We then hit the beach for awhile and looked at some local shops. Definitely worth the trip as the ferry only cost 40 euro round trip.
