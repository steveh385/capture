View of Athens

The city of Athens. We spent a few days wandering Athens where we visited some museums and some of the stunning ruins like the Acropolis, which is a beautiful site at night.
First day we arrived and went for dinner in a great little bistro near our Airbnb. Next morning we headed out to the cable car at Lycabettus where we had incredible views of the city.
We ended going on a 3 hour walking tour which was very informative and took us to locations like the Acropolis, Plato's Acadamey ruins and The Prison of Socrates.
